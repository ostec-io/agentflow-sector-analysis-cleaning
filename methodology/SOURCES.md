# Sources

AgentFlow's Industry Resource Scout agent searched the public web for freely available publications across seven knowledge domains within the commercial cleaning and facilities management industry. The scout prioritised government and regulatory bodies, industry associations, standards organisations, and academic sources. Paywalled and login-gated content was skipped.

## Source Selection Criteria

- **Recency:** 2020 onwards preferred, 2023+ ideal. For standards bodies, always the latest published edition.
- **Authority:** Government agencies, regulatory bodies, industry associations, and recognised standards organisations.
- **Accessibility:** Only freely available, publicly downloadable publications. No paywalled books, member-only reports, or login-gated resources.
- **Relevance:** Directly applicable to commercial cleaning operations, not tangentially related material.

## What Was Skipped

Across all seven domains, approximately 19 additional publications were identified but not downloaded because they were behind paywalls, required membership access, or had been superseded by more recent editions. These were documented during the discovery process but are not included in the analysis.
