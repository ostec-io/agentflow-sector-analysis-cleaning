## Prioritized Backlog

### 1. ScopeBid OS
- **Category**: Business Operations, Facilities Management, Standards & Training
- **Problem Statement**: Cleaning and FM contracts are still priced against incomplete scope, square footage, workload, staffing, and risk data, which drives underbids, rebasing, margin erosion, and scope disputes.
- **Target Users**: Building service contractors, FM providers, estimators, bid teams, procurement teams, operations leaders.
- **Value Proposition**: Produces defensible scopes, workloading models, staffing plans, and tender packs faster, with a clear audit trail behind every assumption.
- **Complexity**: Medium-High
- **Market Potential**: High
- **Score**: 10

### 2. ProofClean Assurance Cloud
- **Category**: Cross-domain (Business Operations, Healthcare Cleaning, Facilities Management, Specialist Niches)
- **Problem Statement**: Inspections, audits, service proofs, complaints, and corrective actions are fragmented across forms, spreadsheets, and local tools, making quality hard to prove and failures slow to close.
- **Target Users**: Contract managers, EVS leaders, FM teams, quality managers, customers, regulators, public-sector buyers.
- **Value Proposition**: Creates a single evidence layer for completed work, scores, photos, defects, remediation, and customer-facing assurance, improving renewals, payment confidence, and compliance.
- **Complexity**: Medium
- **Market Potential**: High
- **Score**: 10

### 3. CrewPulse Workforce OS
- **Category**: Cross-domain (Business Operations, Management & Leadership, Facilities Management, Healthcare Cleaning)
- **Problem Statement**: Labor shortages, absenteeism, part-time staffing, language mismatches, and unstable shifts create missed service, poor retention, and constant manual reshuffling.
- **Target Users**: Operations directors, schedulers, branch managers, supervisors, HR teams, contract managers.
- **Value Proposition**: Rebalances crews by skill, language, training, and workload; flags retention risk early; and supports smarter shift redesign, including daytime-cleaning transitions.
- **Complexity**: Medium
- **Market Potential**: High
- **Score**: 10

## Highest-Value Patterns

- The strongest opportunities are cross-domain operating systems, not single-task apps: scope-to-bid, proof-of-service, workforce orchestration, competency management, and safety/compliance control towers.
- Healthcare, public-sector FM, and regulated chemical workflows are the best vertical wedges because they already have explicit standards, recurring audits, and visible failure costs.
- Specialist niches become attractive when framed as caseflow orchestration products rather than generic field-service tools, especially restoration, substrate diagnosis, and verified specialist dispatch.
